# Enterprise Planning System: User Support Guide

Welcome to the Enterprise Planning System! This comprehensive guide is designed to help you navigate the platform, submit your budgets accurately, manage rolling forecasts, and analyze financial reports.

---

## Section 1: System Overview & Navigation

### Platform Login and Access Levels
The planning system supports three primary access tiers for security and data integrity:
- **Input Users (Business Owners)**: Can enter data and submit templates. Access is restricted to their specific Cost Center/Department.
- **Read-Only / Reporting (Senior Leadership)**: Can view all consolidated data, drill down into variances, and run reports, but cannot alter numbers.
- **Admin (FP&A Team)**: Retain full modeling access, can lock/unlock periods, edit logic drivers, and approve workflows.

### Dashboard Orientation
Upon login, your primary modules will appear on the left-hand navigation pane:
1. **My Tasks**: Workflow items requiring your attention (e.g., submitting your departmental budget).
2. **Budget Input**: Access the Annual Budget Template and Departmental Submission forms.
3. **Forecasting**: Access the 12-Month Rolling Forecast to update driver assumptions.
4. **Reporting**: View the Executive Flash Report and Monthly Budget vs. Actual P&L.

### Navigation Shortcuts
- `Ctrl + S` / `Cmd + S`: Save currently entered data.
- `F9`: Re-calculate formulas and variance columns immediately.
- `Alt + ↓`: Open a dropdown list for Cost Centers or GL Accounts.

## Section 2: Budget Submission Walkthrough

### 1. Accessing Your Budget Form
Navigate to **Budget Input > Departmental Submission**. Ensure the correct **Fiscal Year** and **Scenario** (e.g., "Draft Budget") are selected at the top.

### 2. Data Entry & Editing
- **Yellow Cells** indicate input fields. You may only enter data into these cells.
- **Grey/Green Cells** are locked fields or calculated formulas (e.g., Benefits Load or Quarter Totals).
- Use the **GL Account** and **Cost Center** dropdown menus to categorize your expenses.
- Type a descriptive explanation in the **Description / Justification** column for material requests (e.g., New Software, Travel for Conference).

### 3. Attaching Supporting Documentation
If you have a vendor quote or a detailed headcount ROI sheet, click the **Paperclip Icon** next to the specific line item row to upload the supporting `.pdf` or `.xlsx` file.

### 4. Submission Deadlines and Approval
Once your budget is finalized and verifies against the mathematical accuracy rules:
1. Go to the "Submission Status" tracker on your form.
2. Change the dropdown from `In Progress` to `Submitted`.
3. The FP&A team will be notified automatically. 
*Note: Due dates are typically the 15th of month 11 of the preceding Fiscal Year.*

## Section 3: Forecast Update Process

### Understanding Locked vs. Open Periods
In the **Rolling 12-Month Forecast** module:
- Historical months (Actuals) are locked automatically by the system upon the GL close. These cells appear gray and cannot be overwritten.
- Future months remain open for reforecasting.

### Driver Assumption Inputs
Rather than entering flat numbers for every line item, the system utilizes driver-based forecasting:
- **Revenue**: Enter *Unit Volume* and *Average Price*; the system calculates total revenue.
- **Payroll**: Update the *Ending Headcount*; the system uses standard average salary and benefits loading to compute payroll costs.

### Comparing Versions
Select **Compare To: Prior Forecast** in the dropdown header to activate the "Variance ($)" column, which highlights exactly how your new forecast changes the previously committed figures.

## Section 4: Running & Exporting Reports

### Standard Report Library
Navigate to the **Reporting** module to access:
- **Monthly Budget vs. Actual**: The standard P&L view with variance heat-mapping.
- **Retail Operational Impact**: Driver bridge showing volume vs. price impacts.
- **Executive Flash Report**: KPI summary for C-suite distribution.

### Filters and Exporting
- Use the **Slicers** at the top of any report to filter by specific Entities, Departments, or Time Periods.
- Click the **Export** button in the top right to download the view as a formatted `.xlsx` or a flattened `.pdf`.
- **Scheduled Delivery**: Click the clock icon on a report to have the PDF emailed to your inbox on the 3rd business day of every month.

## Section 5: Troubleshooting & FAQs

### Common Error Messages
- **"Validation Error: Number must be positive"**: Ensure you are not entering expenses as negative numbers. The system sign convention utilizes positive numbers for expenses.
- **"Workflow Blocked: Mathematical Inaccuracy"**: Check that your sub-department totals tie out to the required top-line target limit set by FP&A.

### Support Contacts
- **System Access/Password Resets**: Contact IT Helpdesk at `it-support@company.com`.
- **Data Discrepancies / GL Mapping Rules**: Flag a discrepancy to FP&A via the internal ticketing portal.
- **Service Level Agreement (SLA)**: FP&A guarantees a 4-hour response time during the active budgeting cycle.

## Section 6: Glossary

- **Cost Center**: The functional department (e.g., Marketing, IT) responsible for a specific set of expenses.
- **GL Account**: General Ledger category (e.g., 6010 - Travel) used by Accounting to formally track spend.
- **Version / Scenario**: A saved state of data. E.g., "Working Draft v1" vs "Final Approved Budget".
- **Entity**: A legal corporate structure or geographic subsidiary (e.g., US Corp, EU Subsidiary).
