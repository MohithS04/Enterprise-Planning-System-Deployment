# Enterprise Planning System: Quick Reference Card (QRC)

**Top 10 Tasks & Shortcuts for System Users**

| # | Task | Step-by-Step Instructions | Shortcut |
|---|------|---------------------------|----------|
| **1** | **Login & Access Module** | Navigate to `planning.company.com`. Enter SSO credentials. Click on **Budget Input** (Left Pane). | N/A |
| **2** | **Select Correct CC & GL** | Click the **Yellow cell** in the `GL Account` or `Cost Center` column. Click the dropdown arrow to select from active lists. | `Alt + ↓` |
| **3** | **Enter Expense Data** | Click into Q1-Q4 cells. Type the proposed dollar amount. Ensure numbers are entered **positive** (no negative signs for expenses). | `Enter` or `Tab` |
| **4** | **Save Workflow** | Press the save shortcut to commit changes to the database without submitting. | `Ctrl + S` |
| **5** | **Calculate Subtotals** | If subtotals do not update automatically after an entry, force a re-calculation. | `F9` |
| **6** | **Submit Department Budget** | Go to the `Submission Status` sheet. Change dropdown from `In Progress` to `Submitted`. | N/A |
| **7** | **Update Rolling Forecast** | Navigate to **Forecasting**. (Actuals are gray and locked). Update **Yellow cells** for volume, price, and headcount drivers. | N/A |
| **8** | **Attach Invoices/Quotes** | Highlight the expense row. Click the **Paperclip Icon** > Browse > select the `.pdf` > Upload. | `Ctrl + K` (Link) |
| **9** | **Export Report to PDF** | Open any report. Click the **Export** button (top right). Select `.pdf` and `Landscape` > Click Download. | `Ctrl + P` |
| **10**| **Report an Issue (Ticketing)** | Click the **?** Help icon (bottom left). Select `Data Discrepancy`. Fill out the form. SLA is 4 hours. | N/A |

---

### **Key Terminology Reminder**
- **Draft Status**: Visible only to you. You can freely edit.
- **Submitted Status**: Locked for your editing. FP&A is reviewing.
- **Variance ($)**: Current Data minus Prior Data. (Positive = Favorable for Revenue, Unfavorable for Expense).

*For detailed guidance, refer to the full **User Support Guide** or email FP&A at `fpa@company.com`.*
