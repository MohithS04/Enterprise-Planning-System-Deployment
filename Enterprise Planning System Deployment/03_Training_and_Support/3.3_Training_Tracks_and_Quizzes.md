# Enterprise Planning System: Training Materials

## Part 1: Video Script Outline
**Format**: 5–7 Minute Screen-recorded Modules

**Module 1: Navigation & Basics (All Users)**
- **0:00-1:00**: Intro & System purpose (single source of truth).
- **1:00-3:00**: Logging in, navigating the dashboard, and opening templates.
- **3:00-5:00**: Data entry basics (yellow vs. grey cells, shortcuts).

**Module 2: The Budget Submission Process (Business Owners & FP&A)**
- **0:00-2:00**: Accessing the Departmental form and using GL dropdowns.
- **2:00-4:00**: Inputting data, data validation rules (no negatives).
- **4:00-6:00**: Finalizing the workflow (Draft -> Submitted).

**Module 3: Reporting & Analytics (Leadership & FP&A)**
- **0:00-2:00**: Accessing the standard report library.
- **2:00-4:00**: Using slicers (filters) to drill down into unit variances.
- **4:00-5:00**: Exporting to PDF and scheduling delivery.

## Part 2: Role-Based Training Tracks

### Track A: Business Owners / Department Heads (Input Users)
**Goal**: Independence in submitting robust, accurate budgets and driver inputs.
1. Watch Module 1 (Navigation) & Module 2 (Budget Submission).
2. Review the **Quick Reference Card (QRC)**.
3. Pass knowledge check A.

### Track B: FP&A Analysts (Admins)
**Goal**: Proficiency in managing validations, eliminations, and close cycles.
1. Watch all Video Modules (1, 2, 3).
2. Review the **Validation Rule Library & Close Checklist**.
3. Pass knowledge check B.

### Track C: Senior Leadership (Reporting)
**Goal**: Rapid extraction of insights and high-level variance analysis.
1. Watch Module 3 (Reporting & Analytics).
2. Review the **Executive Flash Report** mockups.
3. Pass knowledge check C.
